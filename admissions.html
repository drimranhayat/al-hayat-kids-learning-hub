Admissions • Ages 3-8

Start with age, goal, and a gentle trial.

Admissions should help parents choose a realistic learning path without pressure or unverified claims.

Start InquiryParent Guide
  1. Share age group: 3-4, 5-6, or 7-8.
  2. Choose goal: English, math, Arabic, Islamic studies, stories, quiz, or online support.
  3. Try one short parent-visible learning task.
  4. Confirm teacher details, class length, schedule, pricing, and progress notes before enrollment.
