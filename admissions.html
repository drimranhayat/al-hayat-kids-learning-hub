Admissions overview

The goal is to reduce parent uncertainty before enrollment.

Parents need to know what happens after they inquire. This admissions page turns the process into clear steps: inquiry, level check, trial class, teacher match, schedule, and monthly plan.

Best first action

Book a free trial or level check before choosing a monthly package.

What parents share

Child age, current level, program interest, class format, and schedule preference.

What parents receive

A recommended learning path, schedule option, and next practice goals.

Enrollment steps

From inquiry to confident enrollment.

1. Inquiry

Parent shares the child's age, goals, and preferred program.

2. Level check

The child's current level and learning needs are understood.

3. Trial class

The parent sees teaching style and child comfort before committing.

4. Enrollment

The family chooses the schedule, format, and monthly plan.

Pricing guidance

Publish pricing only when packages are confirmed.

For launch, keep pricing transparent during inquiry rather than publishing unconfirmed numbers. Once capacity and teacher availability are clear, add monthly packages for one-to-one, group, and online classes.

Admissions FAQs

Questions parents ask before enrolling.

Is the trial class free?

The site is structured around a free trial offer. Confirm final trial policy before wide promotion.

Can parents choose one-to-one or group classes?

Yes, the site supports both inquiry paths. Final availability depends on schedule and teacher capacity.

When should we choose a package?

After the level check or trial class, so the package matches the child's learning needs.
