🧭 Practice Method

Five-step worksheet routine

This routine makes worksheets meaningful and prevents messy, rushed work.

Read

Read the task slowly. Circle command words such as match, write, solve, sort or explain.

Try

Let the child attempt first. Do not over-help before the child thinks.

Check

Review answers together. Ask the child to explain one answer.

Correct

Fix mistakes neatly. Praise correction as a learning success.

Extend

Ask one extra oral question or let the child create a similar question.

👨‍👩‍👧 Parent Guidance

How to make worksheets effective

A worksheet is useful only when it leads to thinking, correction and confidence.

Do this

Use short sessions, clear instructions, gentle checking, praise for effort, and a small follow-up question.

Avoid this

Do not overload the child with many sheets, shout over mistakes, or value speed more than understanding.
