Content note

This Grade 1 worksheet bank is original and child-friendly. It is arranged around common early primary skills: phonics, sight words, sentence writing, number sense, addition, subtraction, shapes, plant and animal basics, health, Islamic manners, Arabic letters, safety, creativity, and life skills. It is designed for gradual practice, not pressure. Teachers and parents may adapt questions according to the child’s level.
