# Launch Roadmap

## Phase 1: GitHub Pages MVP

Goal: launch a polished, trustworthy website that helps parents book a trial class.

- Home page with clear brand message
- Program overview sections
- Parent trust and child safety statements
- Free trial inquiry form connected to WhatsApp
- Admissions flow
- FAQs
- SEO title, meta description, and basic schema

## Phase 2: Growth Website

Goal: turn the single-page site into a stronger SEO and conversion platform.

- Separate pages for Quran Learning, Arabic, Islamic Studies, Early Years, Online Classes, and Weekend Programs
- About page with verified teacher profiles
- Blog with parent education content
- Gallery with real approved photos or illustrations
- Pricing page after packages are finalized
- Downloadable lead magnets such as duas charts and Quran practice checklists

## Phase 3: Platform Features

Goal: support recurring revenue and parent retention.

- Online enrollment
- Payment integration
- Parent dashboard
- Student progress tracking
- Attendance tracking
- Downloadable worksheets
- Recorded lesson library
- Email and WhatsApp follow-up automation

## GitHub Build Steps

1. Finalize the WhatsApp number, email address, and brand contact details.
2. Publish the static site on GitHub Pages.
3. Share the live link with trusted parents and collect feedback.
4. Add verified teacher profiles and policies.
5. Create program-specific SEO pages.
6. Add lead magnets and a blog.
7. Upgrade to a booking and payment system when inquiries become consistent.

## Avoid During Launch

- Do not publish fake testimonials.
- Do not claim certifications that are not verified.
- Do not overcomplicate the website before parents can inquire easily.
- Do not add pricing until the class model, teacher capacity, and schedule are clear.
- Do not launch a parent portal until the manual progress reporting workflow is proven.
