Online class FAQs

Questions parents ask about online learning.

Are online classes suitable for young children?

They can be, if sessions are short, interactive, and supported by parents when needed.

What device is needed?

A phone, tablet, or laptop with stable internet can work. The final platform should be confirmed by the business owner.

Can overseas families join?

The site is designed to scale internationally, but timing and teacher availability should be confirmed during inquiry.

How long should an online class be?

Younger children usually benefit from shorter focused sessions. Final class length should depend on age, subject, and attention span.

Do parents need to sit beside the child?

For younger learners, a parent nearby can help with setup and focus. Older children may need less support once routines are clear.

Which platform will classes use?

The website should publish the confirmed platform only after the business owner finalizes the teaching workflow.
