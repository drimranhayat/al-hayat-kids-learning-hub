Parent Inquiry Form

This form works on GitHub Pages by opening your email app with a prepared message. It does not store the message on a private server.

For children’s safety, please avoid sending home address, child photos, passwords, identity documents, or unnecessary private details.

What to include in your message

For online classes

  • Child’s grade or age range
  • Subject or learning area
  • Current difficulty level
  • Preferred time and days
  • Parent’s main learning goal

For website feedback

  • Page link
  • What is not working
  • Device type: mobile or laptop
  • Screenshot if possible

Response expectations

Usual response time

We aim to respond as soon as possible. During busy teaching periods, replies may take longer.

Languages

Parents may write in English or Urdu for easier communication.

Inquiry Process

How your inquiry is handled

A simple process keeps communication clear, respectful, and parent-focused.

Clear • Safe • Helpful

You send details

Parent shares grade, purpose, learning goal, and preferred reply method.

We review need

The inquiry is checked for class suitability, resource support, or website issue type.

Guidance is shared

You receive a helpful reply, suggested page, class option, or next step.

Learning continues

Children continue with worksheets, quizzes, stories, Islamic learning, or classes.

Important child safety reminder

Children should not contact the website independently with private information. A parent, guardian, or teacher should manage all class inquiries, contact messages, and online learning communication.

Contact FAQ

Quick answers before sending

These common answers may help parents decide what to write.

Can I ask about online classes through this page?

Yes. Select “Online Classes Inquiry” in the form and mention your child’s grade, subject, current level, and preferred timing.

Does the form save my message on the website?

No. On this GitHub Pages version, the form prepares an email in your email app. It does not store the message on a private website database.

Can I report a broken link or page error?

Yes. Please include the exact page link and explain what is not working. A screenshot is helpful when available.

Can children send messages directly?

No. For safety, parents or guardians should handle all contact and online class communication.

Can I suggest new worksheets or activities?

Yes. Suggestions for age-appropriate worksheets, activities, stories, and Islamic learning pages are welcome.

Which language should I use?

You may write in English or Urdu. Clear details help us reply more accurately.
