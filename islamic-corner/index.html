Learning Goals

What children learn here

Islamic learning should not be only reading. It should become dua, manners, gratitude, kindness, worship, and daily character.

Weekly Islamic Routine

A gentle 5-day plan for families

Small regular Islamic practice is better than forcing too much memorisation in one day.

MondayLearn one short dua and use it in daily routine.
TuesdayPractise one good manner at home or school.
WednesdayRead one Seerah or prophet lesson and discuss one value.
ThursdayLearn one Name of Allah and make a simple dua.
FridayReview the week: child explains one lesson and one action.
