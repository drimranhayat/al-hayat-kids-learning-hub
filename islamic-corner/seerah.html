Seerah Journey Map

Learn the life journey in order

This timeline keeps children focused on the main sequence without unsupported details or confusing exaggeration.

Early Life → Revelation → Makkah → Hijrah → Madinah → Farewell
🌱

Early Life

Family, trustworthiness, wisdom, work, and noble character before prophethood.

🏔️

Before Revelation

Reflection, worship, and turning away from wrong practices.

🕋

Makkah

First revelation, Tawḥīd, patience, early Muslims, mercy, and trials.

🗺️

Hijrah

Trust in Allah, wise planning, safety, and migration to Madinah.

🌴

Madinah

Masjid, brotherhood, community, learning, worship, and justice.

🌅

Farewell

Final guidance, rights, dignity, gratitude, and following the Prophet ﷺ.

Authenticity Note

Source-aware, child-friendly, and careful

This page avoids invented stories and exaggerated details. It summarizes widely taught Seerah themes and marks Qur’an references where appropriate.

Qur’an references used

  • Qur’an 33:21 — excellent example
  • Qur’an 68:4 — noble character
  • Qur’an 21:107 — mercy
  • Qur’an 96:1–5 — first revelation
  • Qur’an 9:40 — cave during Hijrah

Teaching caution

For classroom teaching, parents and teachers should use reliable Seerah books and authentic hadith collections. This page is a simplified children’s learning aid, not a replacement for scholarship.

No fabrication rule

Children should learn that loving the Prophet ﷺ includes honesty: do not invent stories, fake miracles, exact conversations, or unsupported details.
