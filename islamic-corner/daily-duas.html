Islamic Corner/Daily Duas

Daily Duas • Ages 3-8

Learn one dua in the right moment.

Begin with common duas used in daily routines, taught gently by parents.

Before Eating

بِسْمِ اللهِ

Say it before meals with kindness and repetition.

After Eating

الْحَمْدُ لِلّٰهِ

Connect this habit with gratitude.
