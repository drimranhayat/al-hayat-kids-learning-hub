Authenticity Note

Learn with love, respect, and care

Use this page as a children’s learning aid. Parents and teachers should review pronunciation and deeper meanings with reliable scholars or trusted Islamic books.

Qur’an foundation

The Qur’an teaches that Allah has the Most Beautiful Names and tells believers to call upon Him by them.

Open Qur’an 7:180

Hadith foundation

Authentic hadith reports state that Allah has ninety-nine Names and that whoever learns, preserves, and acts upon them will enter Paradise by Allah’s mercy.

Open Sahih al-Bukhari 7392

Learning caution

This page uses a widely known children’s learning list. Meanings are simplified for children, so deeper theological study should be guided by qualified teachers.
