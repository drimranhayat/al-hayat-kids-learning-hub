This page is intentionally not showing empty video counters. Until real, reviewed lesson cards are ready, parents can use the screen-time guidance below.

Safe screen time

Watch Less, Learn More

No autoplay, no random video browsing, and no unreviewed external embeds.

Ages 3-4 👀

Point and Repeat

If a parent uses a video, keep it very short and ask the child to point, repeat, and move away from the screen.

Ages 5-6 🧩

Say and Match

Pause often. Ask one question, then match the idea with a picture, object, or worksheet.

Ages 7-8 ✍️

Answer and Practise

After watching, the child should explain one idea and complete one offline task.

✅

What Will Be Added Later

Reviewed lesson cards can be added subject by subject: English sounds, counting, Arabic letters, duas, manners, stories, and short practice videos.

Parent Guide
🖨️

After Every Video

Each video should end with one offline task, such as tracing, matching, reading aloud, counting objects, or practising a dua.
