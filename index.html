FAQs

Questions parents usually ask first.

Is this suitable for complete beginners?

Yes. The first step is a level check so the child can start from an appropriate point.

Do you offer online classes?

Yes. We offer online class options, including one-to-one and small group learning, depending on the child’s level, age, and parent preference.

How do parents track progress?

Progress updates can include attendance, lesson focus, practice notes, and next milestones.

Are teacher profiles verified?

Teacher details should be shared clearly with parents, including experience, teaching style, and suitability for the child’s age group.
