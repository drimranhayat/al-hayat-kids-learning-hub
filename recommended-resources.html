✅ Resource Philosophy

Useful resources are guided, safe and purposeful

The best resources are not always expensive or digital. A reading basket, a notebook, flashcards, a routine chart and kind parent guidance can transform learning.

👨‍👩‍👧

Parent-guided

Children learn better when adults preview, guide and discuss resources.

🛡️

Child-safe

Avoid random browsing, unsafe ads, unreviewed videos and personal data sharing.

✍️

Active learning

Every resource should lead to reading, speaking, writing, drawing or practice.

🌙

Values-based

Resources should support manners, honesty, patience, gratitude and responsibility.

Important safety note: For online resources, parents should preview content first, disable autoplay where possible, avoid public comments for children, and never ask children to share personal information.

🧭 Resource Selection Method

Five checks before using any resource

Use this simple method to choose resources wisely.

Purpose

What skill will this resource build?

Age fit

Is it suitable for the child’s age and level?

Safety

Is it free from harmful ads, comments or unsafe prompts?

Activity

Will the child do something after using it?

Review

Can a parent or teacher check the learning?
