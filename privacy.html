Privacy and safety

No child login required.

Children can learn without creating an account or sharing sensitive personal data.

Local progress only

Stars and completed tasks may be stored in the browser’s localStorage on the same device.

No sensitive child data

The learning activities do not require names, photos, exact location, passwords, or child accounts.

Parent supervision

Parents should supervise children ages 3-8, especially during online browsing and device use.
