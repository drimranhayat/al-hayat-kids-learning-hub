Policy Summary

Clear privacy rules for parents

Last updated: 14 June 2026. Please replace contact details and review this policy before final public launch.

🔐

Child privacy commitment

We design the learning pages so children can use reading, activities, worksheets, videos, duas, and parent-guided tools without creating a child account.

  • No child name, child email, or child login is required for the core learning pages.
🔐

Information children provide

The learning pages should not ask children to submit personal details. Interactive features such as stars, completed items, filters, and practice buttons work inside the browser.

  • Parents should guide children not to type personal information into any form.
💾

Local browser storage

Some pages use local browser storage to remember progress on the same device, such as completed worksheets, memorised duas, watched lessons, or parent checklist items.

  • This is stored in the browser and can be cleared by using page reset buttons or browser settings.
📩

Contact and inquiry forms

The Contact and Online Classes pages may allow parents to prepare an email or WhatsApp inquiry. These forms are intended for parent/guardian use only.

  • Parents should share only necessary details such as child age, subject need, preferred schedule, and learning goal.
📩

Online class inquiries

Online class inquiries may include parent name, child age, subject interest, and preferred time so the team can respond appropriately.

  • Do not publish or share student details publicly.
🌐

Third-party links

The site may link to external resources or platforms. External websites have their own privacy practices and should be reviewed by parents before children use them.

  • Parents should supervise all external resources.
🎬

Video and media safety

The video page is designed with safe lesson preview cards. If YouTube or other videos are added later, they should be owned, verified, child-appropriate, and supervised.

  • Avoid random autoplay and unreviewed external videos.
✅

Cookies

This static website does not need account cookies for child learning pages. Browser storage may be used only for on-device progress and interactive preferences such as sound settings.

  • If analytics or embedded tools are added later, update this policy before publishing.
✅

Parent rights

Parents can review how the site works, clear local browser progress, supervise use, and contact the site owner for questions about class inquiries or published content.

  • Use the Contact page for privacy-related questions.
✅

Important note

This policy is a professional starting point for a children’s learning website and should be reviewed according to your local legal requirements before final public launch.

  • If you add analytics, payment forms, third-party accounts, or live student portals, update this policy immediately.
