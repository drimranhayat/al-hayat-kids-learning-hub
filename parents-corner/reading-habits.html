Quick Principles

What matters most in early reading

Children become readers when books feel warm, meaningful, and part of everyday life.

Love first, pressure later never
💬

Talk before reading

Picture talk and prediction prepare the child’s mind for vocabulary and meaning.

🌙

Use short routines

Ten minutes daily is more useful than one long forced session.

⭐

Praise effort

Praise attention, trying, listening, and retelling, not only perfect reading.

🏡

Make books visible

A small reading corner makes stories part of the child’s home environment.

Weekly Reading Routine

A simple weekly plan for parents

Use this routine with stories from the Reading Library, Islamic stories, worksheets, and oral discussion.

MondayRead aloud + picture walk.
TuesdayChild repeats lines + one new word.
WednesdayRead Islamic or moral story + discuss value.
ThursdayRetell in three sentences + draw scene.
FridayReview favourite story + celebrate progress.
