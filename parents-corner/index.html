How should parents use this website?

Choose an age, pick one daily path, sit nearby for younger children, and celebrate small wins.

Is audio required?

No. The website does not rely on audio, autoplay, or hover sound. Visible text alternatives are provided.

Does the site collect child data?

No child login is required. Progress can be stored locally in the browser only.
