Golden Rule

Start small: A short routine done daily is better than a long plan that collapses after two days.

Choose a Study Routine

🌅

Morning 15-Minute Start

2 min dua/intention, 5 min reading, 5 min phonics or math, 3 min school-bag check.

🏫

After-School Reset

10 min rest, 5 min talk, 20 min homework, 5 min correction and praise.

📖

Reading Routine

Picture talk, child reads, parent reads, then one question and one new word.

✍️

Worksheet Routine

Read instructions, attempt independently, correct gently, explain one answer aloud.

❓

Quiz Routine

Take a short quiz, review mistakes, retry three weak questions, celebrate effort.

🌙

Bedtime Values Routine

Gratitude talk, short story or dua, forgive and sleep peacefully.

Weekly Balanced Plan

MondayEnglish reading + handwriting.
TuesdayMath game + one worksheet.
WednesdayScience observation + drawing.
ThursdayArabic or Islamic Studies + one good deed.
FridayGeneral Knowledge quiz + story retelling.
WeekendRevision, family reading, light project or outdoor learning.

When the child resists studying

Reduce the task

Say: “Let us do only the first two questions together.”

Give choice

Ask: “Do you want reading first or math first?”

Use timer

Try 10 focused minutes, then a short movement break.

Praise correction

Say: “I like how you corrected your mistake.”
