FAQ • Ages 3-8

Parent questions, clear answers.

Simple guidance for safe, parent-guided learning.

Is a child login required?

No. The core learning pages do not require a child login.

What ages is this site for?

The site is focused on ages 3-8 using Ages 3-4, Ages 5-6, and Ages 7-8.

Should parents supervise?

Yes. Younger children should learn with a parent nearby, especially for screen time and reading support.

Does the site use autoplay?

No. The website avoids autoplay and random external video browsing.
