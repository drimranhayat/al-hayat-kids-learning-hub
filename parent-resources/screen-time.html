Parent Guide

Safe Screen Time for Ages 3-8

Use screens for short, purposeful learning. Every online activity should lead to speaking, moving, writing, drawing, reading, or practising offline.

Ages 3-4 🌱

Very Short Sessions

Use one tiny task at a time: point, repeat, name a color, trace one shape, or listen to one short story with a parent.

Ages 5-6 ⭐

Pause and Practise

Pause often. Ask one question, match one picture, count real objects, or repeat one dua before moving to another activity.

Ages 7-8 🚀

Explain and Apply

Ask the child to explain one idea, complete one worksheet, read aloud, or write a short answer after the screen activity.

✅

Simple Rule

One screen task should always be followed by one real-world task. This keeps learning active and parent-guided.

🎥

Video Safety

Use reviewed videos only. Avoid autoplay, random browsing, unreviewed playlists, and long passive watching.
