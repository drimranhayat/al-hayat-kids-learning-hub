Authenticity and Safety Note

Original stories, safe facts, reliable learning direction

The stories are original and child-friendly. Animal facts are simple, educational, and aligned with reliable child-safe animal learning sources.

Reliable animal learning sources

  • National Geographic Kids Animals
  • Britannica Kids Animal Kingdom
  • San Diego Zoo Wildlife Explorers

Child safety rule

Children should observe animals safely, never tease animals, never touch unknown animals, and always ask an adult before helping wildlife.

Nature-care rule

Clean habitats, safe paths, careful water use, and gentle behavior teach children that Earth is a shared home.
