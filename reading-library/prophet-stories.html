🧑‍🏫 Teaching Method

Five-step Prophets' story lesson

Use this simple method so the child does not only hear a story but also understands and practises the value.

Name the Prophet

Say the Prophet’s name respectfully and ask what the child already knows.

Read the summary

Read slowly. Keep the tone warm and avoid frightening exaggeration.

Find the value

Ask: “Which value did this Prophet teach us?”

Connect to life

Ask: “Where can we practise this at home or school?”

Do one mission

Choose one action: say sorry, make dua, share, wait, tell the truth or help.

⭐ Parent & Teacher Promise

Tell Prophets' stories with respect and responsibility

These stories should strengthen love for Allah, respect for the Prophets and practical good manners.

  • Use respectful language for all Prophets.
  • Do not add dramatic invented details to make children afraid.
  • Keep one clear moral lesson per session.
  • Let children ask questions kindly and safely.
  • Connect every story to one practical habit.
  • Use authentic Islamic sources and qualified teachers for deeper study.
