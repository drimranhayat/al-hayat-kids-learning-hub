Source and Safety Note

Authentic, careful, child-friendly use

This page uses careful summaries for children. It does not replace Qur’an recitation, tafsir study, or teacher guidance.

Qur’an-based story references

  • People of the Cave — Surah Al-Kahf 18:9–26
  • Luqman’s advice — Surah Luqman 31:12–19
  • Owners of the Garden — Surah Al-Qalam 68:17–33
  • Army of the Elephant — Surah Al-Fil 105:1–5
  • People of the Ditch — Surah Al-Buruj 85:4–9
  • Charity example — Surah Al-Baqarah 2:261

Hadith-value and everyday stories

Hadith-value cards are child-friendly lessons based on well-known meanings such as good words being charity and removing harm from the road being charity. Everyday stories are original educational scenarios designed to teach Islamic manners without inventing sacred details.
