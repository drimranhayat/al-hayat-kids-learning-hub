Parent Safety Guide

How to use recommended resources wisely

Good resources become better when parents set purpose, time, and reflection.

Purpose before screen time
🎯

Set one purpose

Before opening a site, tell the child the goal: read one story, learn three facts, practice one sound, or solve one task.

⏰

Use a time limit

Short sessions are better for young children. Balance screens with handwriting, reading aloud, drawing, and movement.

🛡️

Protect privacy

Children should not create accounts, post comments, upload photos, or share personal information without parent approval.

💬

Ask reflection questions

  • What did you learn?
  • What was new?
  • Can you explain it in your words?
📝

Turn learning into work

After using a site, ask the child to draw, write three facts, complete a worksheet, or tell a short summary.

🔎

Review external content

External websites may change. Parents should review content, ads, comments, videos, and privacy settings regularly.

Balanced Weekly Use

A simple resource routine

Use this routine to keep online learning purposeful and balanced.

MondayReading or phonics resource + 10 minutes read-aloud.
TuesdayMath or early learning practice + handwritten worksheet.
WednesdayIslamic learning resource + one good deed action.
ThursdayScience or GK resource + draw and label one idea.
FridayReview week: child explains five things learned.

Important external link note

These resources are suggested for parent-guided learning. Al-Hayat Kids Learning Hub does not control external websites. Parents should review privacy policies, ads, account requirements, and content suitability before children use them.
