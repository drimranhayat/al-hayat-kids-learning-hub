Today’s Islamic Learning

Small daily practice, beautiful lifelong habits

Children learn best through small repeated activities. Start with one dua, one value, and one simple question every day.

🤲 Dua of the Day

بِسْمِكَ اللّٰهُمَّ أَمُوتُ وَأَحْيَا

Transliteration: Bismika Allahumma amutu wa ahya.

Meaning: O Allah, in Your name I die and I live.

🌱 Value of the Day

Honesty

A Muslim child should speak the truth, keep promises, and avoid cheating. Honesty makes people trust us.

🧠 Quick Quiz

Which pillar of Islam is performed by fasting in Ramadan?

📌 Parent Tip

Choose one dua for the week. Read it together before sleeping, explain the meaning, and praise the child for every effort.

Learning Pathway

A simple path for children

Keep Islamic learning gentle, practical, and connected with daily life.

1

Learn

Start with a short dua, story, hadith, or manners lesson.

2

Practice

Repeat the lesson through speaking, writing, quizzes, or family discussion.

3

Live

Apply the value at home, at school, with friends, and in daily routines.
