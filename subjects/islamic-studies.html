Open: 8 Islamic Learning Worlds curriculum map

The full curriculum is built around eight child-friendly Islamic worlds. Each age receives the correct level of these worlds gradually.

  • Use one level for several weeks before moving forward.
  • Do one lesson at a time; do not overload the child.
  • Audio is now short: lesson title, key phrase, story, quiz, or mission only.
  • Games are simple because ages 3–7 learn best through small choices and repetition.
  • For exact Qur’an/tajweed and duas, add real recorded MP3 audio later.
