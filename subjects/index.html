Balanced Weekly Routine

A simple subject plan for parents

Do not overload children. A balanced week is better than too many worksheets in one day.

MondayEnglish reading + 10 minutes handwriting.
TuesdayMath games + one worksheet activity.
WednesdayScience observation + drawing or labeling.
ThursdayArabic / Islamic Studies + one good deed.
FridayGeneral Knowledge quiz + story reading.
