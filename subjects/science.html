Parent guidance

Keep science safe and observable.

Use household examples. Avoid risky experiments. Ask children: What do you see? What changed? What should we do next?

Simple experiment

Put one dry seed on wet cotton. Watch it daily with your child. Do not let young children handle small seeds without supervision.
