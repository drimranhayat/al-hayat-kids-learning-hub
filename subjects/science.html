Science curriculum method and map

The child area stays focused on selected age and lesson. Parent guidance is placed here, away from the main learning space.

Open: How young children should learn Science

Ages 3–7 should learn science through observation, safe experiments, real objects, pictures, questions, drawing, sorting, comparing, measuring, and explaining in simple words.

👀

Observe

Look carefully at body, animals, plants, water, weather, light, and objects.

❓

Ask

Use simple questions: What is it? What changed? What do you notice?

🧪

Try Safely

Use safe home materials with adult guidance only.

✍️

Draw & Explain

Children remember better when they draw, label, and explain.

Open: Science learning worlds curriculum map

The curriculum gradually covers senses, body, health, animals, plants, water, air, weather, materials, floating and sinking, magnets, light, sound, forces, space, energy, environment, and safe scientific thinking.

  • Use one age level for several weeks before moving forward.
  • Do one lesson at a time; do not overload the child.
  • Audio is short: fact, observation, experiment, or answer only.
  • Children should observe real objects before memorizing scientific words.
  • Mix safe home items: water, cups, spoons, paper, leaves, toys, magnets, torchlight, fruits, and stones.
