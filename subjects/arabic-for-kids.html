For Parents & Teachers

How to make Arabic enjoyable for children

Arabic learning should feel warm, encouraging, and playful. Focus on a few sounds at a time, and revisit them often in short sessions.

  • Use very short sessions: 5–10 minutes is enough for many young learners.
  • Begin with listening and repeating before expecting accurate writing.
  • Use visuals and real objects where possible.
  • Let the child say the letter, then the word, then the phrase.
  • Use the “I learned this” buttons only after genuine practice.
