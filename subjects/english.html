Parent guidance

Keep English practice short and cheerful.

For ages 3-8, ten focused minutes can be better than a long worksheet. Ask the child to point, say, trace, read, and then celebrate one small win.

Suggested routine

  • Ages 3-4: picture word + sound
  • Ages 5-6: letter + phonics + sight word
  • Ages 7-8: sentence + reading question
