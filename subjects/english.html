English curriculum method and map

The child area stays focused on selected age and lesson. Parent guidance is placed here, away from the main learning space.

Open: How young children should learn English

Ages 3–7 should learn English through listening, repetition, phonics, picture vocabulary, short stories, speaking practice, sentence patterns, handwriting, games, and daily use.

👂

Listen

Hear the sound, word, sentence, and story before writing.

🗣️

Speak

Repeat clearly, answer gently, and use short sentences.

📖

Read

Move from pictures and sounds to words, sentences, and stories.

✍️

Write

Trace letters, copy words, and slowly build independent sentences.

Open: English learning worlds curriculum map

The curriculum gradually covers listening, speaking, alphabet, phonics, vocabulary, sight words, CVC words, rhyming, sentences, punctuation, grammar, reading comprehension, storytelling, handwriting, and creative writing.

  • Use one age level for several weeks before moving forward.
  • Do one lesson at a time; do not overload the child.
  • Audio is short: sound, word, sentence, story, or answer only.
  • Children should listen and speak before writing long text.
  • Mix home items: books, toys, fruits, labels, flashcards, colors, pictures, and storybooks.
