About

A polished learning app for children ages 3-8.

Al-Hayat Kids Learning Hub is designed around one child task per screen, parent guidance, safe Islamic content, and visible learning without child login.

Mission

Help children learn, read, play, and grow through safe Islamic and academic learning.

Values

Trust, kindness, structure, authenticity, accessibility, and parent partnership.

Promise

No fabricated religious claims, no child login requirement, and clear parent guidance.
