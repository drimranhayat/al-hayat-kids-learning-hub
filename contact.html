Contact FAQs

Helpful answers before you send an inquiry.

Can I ask questions before booking a trial?

Yes. Parents should feel comfortable asking about level, schedule, teacher match, class format, and pricing before enrolling.

What if I am outside Pakistan?

Share your country and time zone. The online class page is designed for international Muslim families, subject to teacher availability.

Can I ask about more than one child?

Yes. Send age and current level for each child so each one can be assessed separately.

Can I choose only one subject?

Yes. Parents can begin with Quran, Arabic, Islamic studies, or online classes, then add more later if the schedule is manageable.
