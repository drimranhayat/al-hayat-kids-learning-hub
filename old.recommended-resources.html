Parent Strategy

Do not use everything at once

The best resource is the one your child can use calmly and consistently.

Beginner Reader

Start with Reading Library, then phonics worksheets.

Needs Practice

Use Worksheets with a 15-minute routine.

Low Motivation

Use Activities first, then a short worksheet.

Islamic Routine

Choose one dua and one adab habit weekly.

🌿 Safe Resource Checklist

Before adding any outside resource, parent or teacher should check it carefully.

  • Age suitable: Content matches child age and maturity.
  • No random ads/autoplay: Avoid unreviewed video suggestions.
  • Clear learning purpose: Resource teaches one useful skill.
  • Parent supervised: Young children should not browse alone.
