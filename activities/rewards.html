Reward Rules for Parents

💚

Praise effort first

Reward trying, correction, patience and honesty—not only perfect answers.

🧠

Keep it healthy

Stars should encourage learning. They should not become bribery or pressure.

🤲

Connect with values

Celebrate reading, helping, kindness, prayer manners and gratitude.

🧸

Use small rewards

Choose non-digital rewards: story time, stickers, drawing, family walk, or praise note.
