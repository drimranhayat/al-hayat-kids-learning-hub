How Difficulty Grows

The quiz bank is arranged so learners move from recall and recognition to reasoning, comparison and application.

🌱 EasyBasic words, simple facts, counting, recognition and everyday manners.
🌼 MediumShort reasoning, sentence use, plant/body facts, Arabic vocabulary and daily Islamic learning.
🚀 ChallengingPatterns, concepts, comparisons, grammar choices, geography and thoughtful scenarios.
🏆 MasterHigher-level practice for confident kids: multi-step math, deeper science and broader GK.
