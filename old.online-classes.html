How It Works

A clear parent journey

Simple steps help parents understand the process before committing.

1. Parent Inquiry

Share child age, level, goal, and preferred schedule.

2. Level Check

Assess starting point, confidence, attention span, and learning need.

3. Class Plan

Recommend subject, class type, teacher fit, and practice routine.

4. Progress Updates

Share what was covered, what improved, and what to practise next.

Important

Publish honestly

  • Do not publish unverified teacher credentials.
  • Do not promise fixed results or guaranteed outcomes.
  • Confirm fee, timing, and teacher availability before enrollment.
  • Keep parent communication respectful and transparent.

Suggested class rhythm

Short, consistent sessions with parent-visible practice goals are better than long, irregular classes for children.

🌿 Parent-friendly class principles

Online classes should be safe, calm, structured, and age-aware.

  • Short attention-friendly tasks for younger learners.
  • Gentle correction without fear or embarrassment.
  • Parent notes after class where possible.
  • Clear home practice that parents can support.
